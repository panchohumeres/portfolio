metAtlas: Metropolitan Atlas from social media<

Work done as Project Assistant, at the MIT Media Lab / Macro Connections Group (guided by prof. César Hidalgo). Work included the download and pre-processing of twitter geo-located data for 50 Metropolitan Areas around the world (7-8 million tweets), the development of a socio-spatial Clustering algorithm for definition of urban areas (based on Spectral Analysis of networks), and the Assistance in the writing of proposals for securing 30K+ US$ in funding for the project (MIT MISTI-Chile Grant).
