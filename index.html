Dynamo-covid World [2021]

WORLD COVID-19 Tracking Panel, synced with OWID (Our World in Data) data.

Dynamo-covid [2020]

Chile COVID-19 Pandemic Monitoring Panel, based on native "superJupyter" stack -Apache Superset, Jupyter Notebooks and PostreSQL-.

panchoMan [2020]

Personal handbook for several technologies (in Spanish), made with python-sphinx

NACK Search [2020]

Replica search engine for the Chilean Government open data portal, based on open source-software CKAN. Search engine made with Elasticsearch, built with public and open data sources.

State of Open Data [2019]

Dashboard for visualizing real-time stats of the Chilean Government Open Data portal (datos.gob.cl), based on Kibana

eigenCities [2015-17]

R&D project funded by the Chilean Government Scientific Agency (CONICYT). 200K+ in funding for the development of urban clustering algorithms applied to mobile communications data, and associated web and wall-display interfaces for aiding the exploratory research process.

Dissecting Urban Boundaries [2017]

Exploratory research on delineation of City Limits from mobility data in chilean Urban Areas.

Urban Power Centrality [2013]

Thesis project at MIT. Network analysis applied to mobility data derived from a dataset of 242.000 tweets in Greater Santiago, for finding Central Places.

metAtlas [2013-14]

Atlas of worldwide Urban Areas made from 7-8 MM records of Twitter data.
